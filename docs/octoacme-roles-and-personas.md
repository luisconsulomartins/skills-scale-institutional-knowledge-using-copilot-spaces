# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers own the user experience strategy, design validation, prototyping, and usability feedback. They ensure that OctoAcme's products are intuitive, accessible, and meet user needs.

### Responsibilities
- Design user interfaces and user workflows based on research and user feedback
- Create prototypes and mockups to validate design decisions
- Conduct usability testing and gather user feedback
- Collaborate with Product Managers to translate product goals into user-centered designs
- Work with Developers to ensure technical feasibility of designs
- Document design systems, patterns, and guidelines for consistency

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, user-friendly experiences that drive adoption
- Reduce friction in workflows through thoughtful design
- Ensure accessibility and inclusivity in all designs

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and feedback sessions with product and engineering teams
- Usability testing reports and user research summaries
- Design documentation and component libraries
- Collaboration in sprint planning to discuss design constraints and opportunities

### Interaction with Other Roles
- **Product Managers**: Align on user needs, success metrics, and design priorities
- **Developers**: Discuss technical feasibility, performance, and implementation details
- **QA Engineers**: Ensure design intent is correctly implemented and validated

---

## QA Engineer

### Role Summary
QA Engineers own the creation and execution of test strategies, test plans, and quality assurance processes. They ensure that deliverables meet acceptance criteria and maintain high quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Create and execute comprehensive test plans (manual and automated)
- Develop and maintain automated test suites and CI/CD quality checks
- Document test cases and maintain traceability to acceptance criteria
- Identify, triage, and report defects with clear reproduction steps
- Validate fixes and ensure quality before release
- Partner with teams to define testability requirements and acceptance criteria

### Goals
- Prevent defects from reaching production
- Ensure test coverage aligns with risk and complexity
- Enable fast, confident releases through automation and clear quality metrics

### Typical Communication
- Test plan reviews and quality metrics dashboards
- Defect reports with reproduction steps and severity assessment
- Acceptance criteria validation and sign-off
- Collaboration with Developers on testability and test strategy

### Interaction with Other Roles
- **Developers**: Collaborate on test strategies, help identify edge cases, and validate fixes
- **Product Managers**: Clarify acceptance criteria and validate that features meet requirements
- **Project Managers**: Report quality metrics and coordinate release readiness

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts bridge the gap between business requirements and project delivery. They elicit, clarify, and document user needs, translate them into actionable requirements, and ensure alignment with business objectives.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct stakeholder interviews and requirements elicitation
- Document user stories, use cases, and functional and non-functional requirements
- Clarify ambiguous or conflicting requirements with stakeholders
- Validate that proposed solutions address business problems
- Support impact analysis for changes and dependencies
- Document acceptance criteria and success metrics

### Goals
- Ensure projects deliver business value and solve real problems
- Reduce rework due to misaligned or missing requirements
- Enable clear communication between business and delivery teams

### Typical Communication
- Requirement specification documents and user stories
- Stakeholder interviews and feedback summaries
- Impact analysis and change request documentation
- Collaboration in planning and retrospectives

### Interaction with Other Roles
- **Product Managers**: Align on business priorities, user needs, and success metrics
- **Project Managers**: Support scheduling, risk identification, and stakeholder coordination
- **Developers**: Clarify technical feasibility of requirements and support design discussions
- **UX Designers**: Collaborate on user workflow validation and usability requirements
